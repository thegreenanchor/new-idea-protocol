# Phase 2: Targeted Interview

Classify maturity and type, then ask no more than three blocking questions.

Question areas:

- Core user flow.
- User/problem.
- MVP boundary.
- Data/storage.
- Auth/access.
- Integrations.
- Technical complexity.
- Technology decisions.

Prompt:

```text
I will ask up to three blocking questions, one at a time. If anything remains unclear after that, I will mark it as an assumption, decision required, deferred, or out of scope.
```
