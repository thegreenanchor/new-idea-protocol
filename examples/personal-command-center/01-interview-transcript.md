**Agent:** I will run this through New Idea Protocol before any code is written. First classification: this is a solution-first personal tool and AI-agent workflow. What is the success moment after someone records a voice note?

**Agent:** Good. What is the smallest useful MVP, without calendar sync, team sharing, or a mobile app?

**User:** Upload or paste one transcript, get a brief, assumptions, decisions, and next actions as Markdown.

**Agent:** What data must persist in v1?

**User:** The original transcript, generated brief, assumptions, decisions, and export history. Local files are fine for now.
